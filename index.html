Unit 4 Game


Word Game - but not really

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four buttons below. By clicking on a button you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.
You win the game by making your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each button is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each button.
Press reset to reset the game.
Press STOP to exit to game to the browser.
